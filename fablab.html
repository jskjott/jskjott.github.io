Fablab Seoul

Being a Maker In-residence

Fablab Seoul is a wondrous place. Nothing expresses this better than the short video I made to document the environement there:

While I was there I decided I wanted to make a portable, battery-operated receipt printer typewriter inspired by a similar project done by Robert Ochshorn. Disillusioned with contemporary practices in which all editing and layout work is done on the computer on programs such as InDesign I used a keyboard and a receipt printer to make a typewriter which allowed me to print out what I was writing at the moment of writing.

Computer-based editing lacks many of the affordances working in a physical environment brings. Text printed on paper can be cut out, glued back together, and drawn on. The typewriter became an object which incentivised play and improvisation.
