Engaging Forms for Participation

Service design research

July 2018 -> Sep 2018

Design research on improvement of agricultural research methodology with Sebatian Deterding, Jeske Van de Gevel and my fellow intern Niamh Murchan. I went to Kenya to do field testing of both paper and experience prototypes.

Project Outcomes

Proposed next iterations of observation and feedback forms

Repository of tested group exercises

A collection of custom icons

Graph literacy assessment data for 17 participants

Full documentation of field days (audio, photos, videos)

Field report

Report on prototyping process

Annotated portfolios for feedback and obervation card prototypes

How we spent our time

Part of the research involved exploring how our users understood different representations of data, known as their graph literacy. To get a good understanding of this we designed an simple assessment in which we asked our users to explain to us how they understood different visualisations.
